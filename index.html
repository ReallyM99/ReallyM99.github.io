Days of Fresh Coffee and Pastries

Our coffee is awaiting for you!

Here at Sunshine Latte, coffee is just the beginning! Our team of five passionate baristas and bakers craft irresistible treats daily, including our famous caramel-topped, cranberry-filled muffins.

We’re also a pet-friendly spot, so bring your furry friends along and enjoy your coffee with some tail wags or purring companionship.

Feeling creative? Join our muffin-making classes or weekend workshops! From crochet Fridays and painting Saturdays to family fun on Sundays, there’s always something exciting happening. Sunshine Latte is more than a café – it’s a place where coffee, creativity, and community come together!
